Reformed Family Feud Survey Results

I host a monthly game night at my church. We are a small Reformed Baptist congregation in Southern Ohio. Ususally, game night consists of a selection of my board games and a couple TVs with video game systems set up on them. The purpose of game night is to give our congregants a night each month where we can get to know each other better, all for teh Glory of God.

Twice I have run a "Bad Game Show" instead of our normal time of general gaming.
